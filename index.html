How it works

Four steps. You in charge.

Every engagement follows the same rhythm — structured, transparent, and built so you leave with more than a working system.

01

Listen & Diagnose

A structured first session to map your systems, surface real blockers, and define what's worth solving first. No sales pitch — honest diagnosis.

You walk away with: a clear, honest diagnostic

02

Transparent Plan

A roadmap you understand and own — clear tradeoffs, realistic timelines, priorities set together. Even if we stop here, the plan is yours to keep.

You walk away with: a roadmap you actually own

03

Build Together

Side by side, not from a distance. Your team is inside every key decision. Every choice gets explained as we make it — not buried in a doc written after the fact.

You walk away with: a system your team understands

04

You Take the Wheel

Real handover — live walkthroughs, Q&A, recorded sessions. Your team doesn't just receive the system: they know how to drive it, fix it, and evolve it without me.

You walk away with: full autonomy — zero dependency

Our approach

We don't just deliver.
We transfer.

Most consultants hand over a finished system and leave. Lightboat stays alongside your team — explaining decisions, building internal knowledge, and making sure your people can own what we build together.

We believe technology only creates lasting value when the people using it understand it. That's why every engagement includes structured knowledge transfer and honest education, not just a handoff document.

Accompagnement réel

We embed with your team, not just consult from a distance. Slack, stand-ups, code reviews — we're part of the process.

Honest timelines

No surprises. We plan carefully, communicate early, and adjust together when things change.

Education first

Every delivery includes the "why." Your team leaves with skills and confidence, not just a working system.

Practical over fashionable

We recommend tools that fit your team, not the latest hype. Sustainable beats spectacular.
