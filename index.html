Our approach

We don't just deliver.
We transfer.

Most consultants hand over a finished system and leave. Lightboat stays alongside your team — explaining decisions, building internal knowledge, and making sure your people can own what we build together.

We believe technology only creates lasting value when the people using it understand it. That's why every engagement includes structured knowledge transfer and honest education, not just a handoff document.

Accompagnement réel

We embed with your team, not just consult from a distance. Slack, stand-ups, code reviews — we're part of the process.

Honest timelines

No surprises. We plan carefully, communicate early, and adjust together when things change.

Education first

Every delivery includes the "why." Your team leaves with skills and confidence, not just a working system.

Practical over fashionable

We recommend tools that fit your team, not the latest hype. Sustainable beats spectacular.
